An older gentleman who owns and runs the general store in Phandalin, he's quite a nervous individual and typically very soft-spoken, preferring a quiet night in over the noise and bustle of the local tavern.

Though he typically enjoys interactions with adventures - many have assisted him in recovering stolen goods from thieves that robbed his stores in his younger years - recent events have left a little frazzled.
