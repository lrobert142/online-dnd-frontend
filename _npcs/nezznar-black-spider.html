The mysterious figure behind the Redbrand's presence in Phandalin. They seem to have a deep interest in the Phandelver mine, though what for or why remains completely mystery.

The party encountered the nefarious Black Spider deep in the Phandelver Mine after a vicious fight to the mysterious leader. Though his deception and disarming charisma was enough to establish himself amongst his goblin allies, he was not able to best the party. Even his cowardly attempts to run and hide were thwarted by the party's keen perception.

Bound, gagged, and dragged back to Phandalin to face justice for his crimes, he is now awaiting a lengthy prison sentence in Neverwinter. May he never see the light of day again.

Strangely enough, he seems to have trouble recalling his name, able only to babble nonsense about it being taken from him.
