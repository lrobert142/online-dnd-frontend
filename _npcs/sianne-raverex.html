The queen of Andoril who rules alongside her husband, King Aldis, in Baldur's Gate. She has ruled alongside her husband for just under twenty years and is known to be direct, and firm but fair. She is highly regarded as a shrew diplomat who is able to smooth over deals many others would dread to ever be challenged with.
