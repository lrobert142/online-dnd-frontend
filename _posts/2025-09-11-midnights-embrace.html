Crafted from the rare Shadeberries, grown only in the moonlit groves of the Winter Court, where a thin shell of frost protects the maturing fruit, allowing it to develop the signature sweet flavour.

When uncorked, it releases a cool, sweet scent frost-kissed fruit, and a hint of winter rose.

The first sip coats the tongue in a silken warmth, and each one after blossoms a subtle heat that grows throughout the body. When touched by one with fey blood, that warmth takes root in one's desires, leading to bonds, or mistakes, that last far beyond the one night.
