These tiny golden birds native to the Feywild are found everywhere commerce is found, from solo market stalls in tiny villages to large trade districts in major cities. They adore coins of all kinds and those who are not careful around them will find them more than willing to swoop in and steal some right from an unsuspecting target's hand.

Nobody quite knows why they insist on stealing these coins, though there have been many theories such as that they are actually small dragons in disguise trying to build their own hoards. Or that they believe the coins are actually their young or their eggs and are returning them to their nests to raise and hatch.

Others again postulate that these creatures are actually agents of Asmodeus sent to collect tithes of riches to their demonic lord in exchange for unlimited power and will one day overthrow the Archfeys and rule the Feywilds!

Regardless of why they do what they do, or indeed where they even come from, one cannot deny there is a certain cuteness to their looks and actions, and many adventurers have been duped into taking their eyes off their coin-pouches for just long enough to find them suspiciously a few coins lighter.

But just look at the darlings, they wouldn't do that now would they? No surely not, so please, just look at the pretty birdy for a moment longer, and ignoring the jingling in your purse.
