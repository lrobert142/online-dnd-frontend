A coalition of adventurers and helpers from all across Navirar, these good-doers stand for freedom and self-expression and vehemently oppose all sorts of evil and frequently stand against it at every opportunity. From tyrants to evil mages, minor inconvenience to end-of-the-world plots, The Harpers are always seen where they are needed most offering a much-needed helping hand to those who need it most.

Many adventurers join the ranks of The Harpers and every major settlement in Navirar has at least one Harper Outpost, a building where fellow Harpers may catch up with each other and share tales of their latest heroic deeds. In fact, anyone can join The Harpers, from the simplest farmer to the mightiest warrior, all are welcome and respected as equals and will be named an Associate, the highest (and only) rank; after all, freedom belongs to everyone, so why bother with meaningless titles and advancement?

As an organisation, they pledge allegiance to Tymora as they believe everyone needs a little luck in their lives to help them make the best of a bad situation or capitalize on a good one. It is worth noting however that they have no issues with others having different gods or indeed none at all.
