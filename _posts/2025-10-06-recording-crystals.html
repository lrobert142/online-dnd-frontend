--- layout: post banner: assets/img/lore/recording-crystal-banner.jpeg title: Recording Crystals excerpt: A simple gem imbued with magic that allows it to record a voice and be replayed with perfect clarity later. Useful for relaying important messages. ---

Information is critical to many aspect of life, whether adventuring, exploring, commanding troops in battle, or even just remembering what to buy at the markets. However, memories are fallible, notes can struggle to carry intonation, and Sending spells are not always readily available. As such, recording crystals were created to provide a simple to use, readily available means to consistently relay information across long distances and multiple recipients.

A truly magnificent invention of Wintexia, the utility of this item is undoubtable and has even managed to become commonplace in Navirar, a true rarity given the inherent distrust Navirar has in Zyxs' technology. It is now produced on both continents with regularity and can be found in all manner of colours, shapes, and sizes with some magic shops even offering customised etchings into the crystal itself and voice modulation options when a message is recorded.

With the prevalence of different types and sizes of crystals, and a never-ending desire for new types of customisation it is no surprise that some magic artisans have taken to imbuing the recording magic in other formats such a coins (colloquially referred to as Whispering Coins), earrings, and even mirrors!

As the world grows and staying in touch with loved ones becomes harder and harder, the demand for new and interesting types of Recording Crystals only continues to grow, as does the creativity of those who make them.
