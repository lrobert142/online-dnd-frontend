--- layout: post banner: assets/img/lore/dawnfires-pulse-banner.png title: Dawnfire's Pulse excerpt: An intense crimson, swirled with ribbons of glittering silver with a smooth, milk-like texture and consistency ---

Brewed from the crushed hearts of Embernuts, a rare fruiting nut only found in the tricky sunlit grassland that borders the numerous beaches of the Summer Court, it is said to hold a fragment of Dawn itself. Steeped in the Moonmilk of the Dairy Fairy (A breed of cows-like creatures with colourful spots, some say it's actually a very kinky tribe of Minotaur-like beings, though this has never been confirmed) and aged in crystal balls made from red sugar crystals, creating an alcoholic beverage that blurs the lines between a cocktail and a sweet cream. Authentically served straight from the brewing ball, it nonetheless retains it's flavours and effects after bottling for ease of export.

As the silky ribbons ripple from the bottle, the scent of roasted almonds and ripe strawberries permeate the room, a welcome sign of the celebration having only just begun. It's taste begins soft and sweet, followed by a nutty note that paves the way for the radiant chill that shoots through the body. The first gulp is always the hardest but once that chill subsides, all that is left is a quickened pulse and a stirred spirit. Those who drink it find themselves with a louder laugh, a lighter step, and courage untempered.

Some say it was brewed to revitalize those who frequent the Summer Court's many beachside parties after hours of endless dancing, others say it grants the drinker a sliver of the Summer King's vitality. Although these gifts are rarely given freely - as it is followed by a regretful crash - a bottle graced by a Fey's presence will instead soothe the aches and pains of a long day.
