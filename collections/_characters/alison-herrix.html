A travelling academic who came to Phandalin with her travel companion, Edward Fluffbottom. Unlike her companion who prefers to learn about the past, she seeks to chronicle the present and help establish a safe and prosperous future. Upop learning of the party's deeds, she requested Gaelin to allow her to build a library to help further her studies.

"The past in already done; it is immutable, unchangeable. Why focus only on that which cannot change when you can experience the here and now and make the future better for everyone?"
