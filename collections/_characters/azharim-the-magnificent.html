A quick-talking, shrewd travelling salesman and proprietor of The Curio Caravan, who works out of his caravan. Nobody is quite sure where his goods come from, why an all-powerful genie is a simple merchant, or how his caravan gets around (as nobody has ever seen it with horses to pull it). He however, claims that all of these questions pale in comparison to the really important one: "What amazing deals does Azharim have for you today?"
