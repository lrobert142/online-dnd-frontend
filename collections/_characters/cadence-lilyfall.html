A hyper, excitable, ditz of a waitress who 'helps' Tiko at the Red-o-roar inn. She doesn't quite seem to understand the gravity of the situation Malgalus is in, but is always happy, kind, and reassuring with gentle words for everyone, raising the spirits of all those around her. Though quite aloof, she always gives 100% effort and goes above and beyond to ensure great customer service. When she remembers what that means...
