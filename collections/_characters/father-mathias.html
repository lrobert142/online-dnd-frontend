Despite his old age, bad knees, and shaky hands, Father Mathias is kind and gentle soul with a very open mind and a helpful demeanour. His faith in the great platinum dragon Bahamut is unshakeable, however he welcomes those from others faiths and enjoys debating philosphy and religion with them, seeing it as a way to expand his horizons and better those in need.

His openness to diversity was first put on display when he agreed to officiate the wedding between Ginger and Mango, even going so far as to spend weeks rewriting his sermons to be inclusive of same-sex couples (despite his aching joints).

Upon seeing Darax join the fight to protect Wolfhaven and learning of the lycanthropes in the nearby woods - and their protection of the town - he was the first to publicly welcome them into the town, seeing them as guardians sent by Bahamuts himself! While that may not have been entirely true, his faith and openness for diversity and acceptance lead to him working alongside Gaelin to develop a new sermon that painted the lycanthropes in a positive light. And it worked fantastically, thanks to Gaelin's incredible assistance.
