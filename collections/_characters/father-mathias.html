Despite his old age, bad knees, and shaky hands, Father Mathias is kind and gentle soul with a very open mind and a helpful demeanour. His faith in the great platinum dragon Bahamut is unshakeable, however he welcomes those from others faiths and enjoys debating philosophy and religion with them, seeing it as a way to expand his horizons and better those in need.

His openness to diversity was first put on display when he agreed to officiate the wedding between Ginger and Mango, even going so far as to spend weeks rewriting his sermons to be inclusive of same-sex couples (despite his aching joints).

Upon seeing Darax join the fight to protect Wolfhaven and learning of the lycanthropes in the nearby woods - and their protection of the town - he was the first to publicly accept them, seeing them as guardians sent by Bahamut himself! While that may not have been entirely true, his faith and openness for diversity and acceptance led to him working alongside Gaelin to develop a new sermon that painted the lycanthropes in a positive light. And it worked fantastically, thanks to Gaelin's incredible assistance!

Hearing heard from numerous lycanthropes their dislike for being made to wear loincloths, Father Mathias confronted Peet about the forced clothing and was able to convince him that if they are to truly understand and accept others, they must also be willing to accept their way of life and clothing choices.

He was relieved to see his words were taken to heart and that the lycanthropes can now truly be how they wish to be; free and happy.
