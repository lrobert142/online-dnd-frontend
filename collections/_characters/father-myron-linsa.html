Head priest of Ilmater's Mercy Church, he is kind, gentle, understanding, and never harshly judges another. He seeks to embody the goodness that Ilmater promises and has unbreakable loyalty to his god. Still, he does let his faith get in the way of others beliefs and will honour their own gods - or lack thereof if they are not religious - provided they show proper respect while in the church itself.
