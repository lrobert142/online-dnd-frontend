A hardworking and very pragmatic individual, Ginger spends her days helping around Wolfhaven wherever she can. Be it helping to harvest wheat and vegetables, milling grains, or cleaning the inn alongside her beloved wife Mango, she is a common face around town. Everyone knows and respects her greatly and even though she might grumble and groan sometimes, she always comes through in the end.

Many agree that Mango has had a noticeable improvement on her otherwise somewhat pessimistic outlook on life and describe them as "soulmates lucky enough to have found each other."

Though normally not one for pets, Sýnchysi's Fey Blinkhound (Fari) seems to have worked its way into her heart and she was more than happy to spend hours of the night playing with him alongside Mango. Despite repeatedly telling Mango not to overfeed him, she couldn't bring herself to stop the flow of treats to Fari seeing how happy it made him.

Seeing her partner cruelly cut down by the cultists who invaded the town, she felt her heart break in two and was unsure how she would be able to continue without her beloved. However, with Gaelin and Peet's help to bring Mango back, Ginger was overcome with happiness and excitement and swore to assist them in any way she could.

The near-death experience of her partner seems to have given her a brighter outlook on life; a sincere wish to enjoy each moment before it's too late.

Thanks to the help of Mango, Petee, and herself, the feast they cooked up was truly magnificent and helped solidify the alliance and trust between the villagers and the lycanthropes. Never did she think she would see a lycanthrope - much less call one a friend - but seeing the festivities and happiness in the air she couldn't help but welcome them with open arms.
