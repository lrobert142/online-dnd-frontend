Known as "Mister Gregory" by most in Wolfhaven, he is the owner of the village's only general store who interacts directly with travelling merchants to get goods the town otherwise does not produce, as well as sell off any excess items the villagers provide to him. Acting as the main means through which the village communicates with the outside world, he is the only one whom people trust their goods and money with, and he always makes sure everyone gets everything back.

Before arriving in town nearly 15 years aho, he was supposedly a travelling merchant himself, spending the vast majority of his life on the road and seeing all manner of amazing and dangerous things. However, after many years he finally deciding to settle down somewhere quiet and retire, happy to finally be able to settle in one location. Although his merchant blood ended up spurring him into creating the town's general store only months after he arrived, much to the town's benefit. At least he doesn't have to travel much.

Ever the heavy sleeper, he thankfully missed the cultist attack on the village, and the talk of lycanthropes in the area. While it at first worried him, hearing firsthand from Petee that they were peaceful protectors did wonders to allay his fears and he was happy to provide some basic materials for the celebration.
