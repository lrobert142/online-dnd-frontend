The Captain of Stalerock Fort, Joanna was a strong and great leader who ensured all those under her command were given their opportunity to reach their potential. Ever the lover of cyphers and riddles, she was known to spend her free time making and solving puzzles with those she was stationed with, helping them think outside the box and training their minds.

She and Sir Anima Whiteclaw had been friends since early childhood, and they always pushed each other to be their very best, forming a close friendship.

Alas, with the invasion of Stalerock Fort by Raemus and the Bhaalists, she and her soldiers stood little chance at stopping the sudden ambush and she was forced to retreat to her quarters. After reusing to speak to Raemus, she was unceremoniously cut down in cold blood.

With the party's success, her body was recovered and she was laid to rest in the yard of her beloved fort beside her loyal soldiers.
