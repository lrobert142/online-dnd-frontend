A grumpy, loud, obnoxious bugbear who is rarely seen without his elderly pet wolf, Snarl. His attitude rarely earns him a lot of friends, but his natural leadership and connections to less-than-savoury individuals could prove useful in defending Thundertree. Not like he would have given you much of a choice, stating (not asking) that he will set up a barracks and stock it with some 'friends' he knows.

"And I ain't doing this because I like you lot, I'm doing it so y'all fuckers don't go round tellin' people the great King Grol don't pay back 'is fuckin' debts!"
