An old friend of Petee's who now runs the best - and only - inn in Wolfhaven, she is always one for rumours and stories, no matter how absurd or outlandish they are. Though she does have a much better fondness for true stories from adventurers and hopes that one day Wolfhaven will grow big enough that adventurers will frequent her inn and regale her with wonderful tales. So far it has only been a few travelling merchant.

She is easy-going, very kind, always sees the good in life no matter how dire a situation seems and refuses to let anyone bring the mood down. Her upbeat, persistent optimism and happy-go-luck view on life - and her soothing excited chirps - can bring a smile to even the most gruff farmer.

She is due to be wed to Ginger Balam soon!
