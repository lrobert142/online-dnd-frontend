One of the captains of the paladins of Redorahh, and second-in-command serving under Sir Anima, Marielle serves the city with a work ethic that puts many others to shame. She has frequently shown herself to be the first to jump into action when situations grow dire, always one to stand on the front-line to defend from outside attacks, and is widely loved by both the people and other guards. Even other captains look up to her as a shining beacon of what a guard should be.

She is also surprisingly calm given wolverines are generally known to have short tempers and a vicious disposition as well as a love of fighting. When asked why she simply shrugged her shoulders and said she was born different. “The world has enough fighting already, why not do something better for it?”
