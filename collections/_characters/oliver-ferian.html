Owner of the Green Thumb Apothecary in Redorahh. A lover of plants of all kinds, they have a near-encyclopedic knowledge about all sorts of herbs, roots, tree, and all things plants. He has a self-admitted green thumb (pun intended, he assures you) and enjoys growing and selling all kinds, both living plants and dried herbs that people can use; be they for adventurers or cooks. He is also a *budding* alchemist (pun also intended) and can make some simple potions though is far from an expert so more complex items are admittedly beyond his ability.

Above all, he wants to work with plants from other planes and would give nearly anything to have the opportunity to grow them in his garden and see them flourish.
