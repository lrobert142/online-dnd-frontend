A monster of a paladin wrapped in solid steel armour, this devotee of Bhaal revels in death with an unnatural glee. Believing all to be nothing more than sacrifices to the great Lord of Murder, they are willing to commit atrocities without a second thought.

Upon being encountered in Stalerock Fort - which they had led a bloody and brutal takeover of - they taunted the party into a fight and attempted to make them the latest sacrifices to the Blood Father. However, even with all their tricks and power, they too fell to the party's justice.

Even in their final moments they smiled and taunted them, welcoming death as they were sent to rest beside their horrific god of death. Let us hope their god is not as merciful as the party.
