Lead hunter of the pack, his insatiable prey drive and incredible stamina ensure that any hunt he partakes in is guaranteed to succeed. Despite his eagerness to hunt though, he has a soft side to him as well and loves to be softer around those he knows he can trust. Unless you act like a brat around him, then he'll happily show off how much he enjoys taming them.

Though he has long since given up tracking his age - as do most lycanthropes - he estimates himself to be at least 500 years old, and has spent the vast majority of those years happily leading his pack to fresh prey. The entire pack looks to him for hunting techniques and respects him greatly.
