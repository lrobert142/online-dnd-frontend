An older gentleman who has spent many decades protecting Ithacos as one of their most decorated guard commanders (a rank above typical guard captains). His mere presence commands respect and pride from all those who serve under and alongside him, and his level head in even the most difficult and dangerous situations has saved many lives.

His age has afforded him much wisdom, but make no mistake he is still very strong and sturdy and is able to take blows that would knock out those half his age.

Called from the capital to check on Redorahh before it shut itself away, he looks forward to the day he can return to Ithacos to see his wife, daughter, and grandson again. Though he rarely speaks of them, when he does it is obvious that he admires them greatly and misses them even more.

Thanks to some shared friends he was temporarily required at the Malgalus-Andoril border where the party met him and was able to convince him they were allies with a strange, large glass diamond. Though he did not elaborate on what it meant - or why he seemed so surprised to see it "after all these decades" - it is clear it is quite significant to him.

He now travels with the party, taking them to Redorahh where they can help fight back against the Bhaalist menace tormenting Malgalus.
