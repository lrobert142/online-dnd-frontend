The middle aged owner of the Red-o-Roar Inn, she has been greatly affected by the lockdown of Malgalus, particularly reducing their supplies and the amount of travellers staying at their inn. Additionally, the recent events and fears of the cultist attacks have made them understandably stressed and worried about the future of Malgalus, though they try to hide it behind faux-confidence when talking with customers. It rarely works, fatigue often evident in their voice.

She hears quite a few rumours and unfortunately very few are good. She thus would love to hear some good news once in a while and would revel in tales of good and victories to soothe her frazzled nerves.
