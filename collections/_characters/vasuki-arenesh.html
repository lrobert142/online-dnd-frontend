A mysterious naga who proclaims to be a powerful enchanter and runs the very unusual store called The Snake Oil Emporium; a shop which exists entirely inside a demiplane and is accessed only through an unusual golden-snake door that appears to show up at random (and disappear just as quickly).

His love of pranks and tricks is evident in the many seemingly pointless enchanted items he makes and sells; from some that sound useful but do nothing, to those that sound useless and very much are. Indeed, many who enter his store do so with the sole idea of purchasing his goods to prank their friends and family. However, he does also have a smaller selection of useful goods and has been known to create custom powerful enchantments. When the mood strikes him.

Aside from his love of pranks, he has a reputation for being an enormous flirt, knowing exactly how to make groups flustered and hot under the collar without making them uncomfortable. Be wary of flirting back with this serpent, he will see it as a challenge and flirt back even harder until you either yield, or end up in his bedroom.

The party purchased a small selection of items from him for very cheap - including the Boots of Water Trekking and Ring of Detecting Magic - along with various magic potions, only to discover they did not do what they wanted them to do! Incensed and feeling cheated, the party confronted the naga when his door next showed up near their camp (which Vasuki insists was just to hand them some items, and definitely not because he is following them) only to be told that at no point had he lied to them; had they asked he would have told them the items were pranks.

Though upset, the party was able to ask if he might be able to create actually useful items for them in the future, which he begrudgingly said he would consider, depending on what it was and his mood at the time. Perhaps there he can be useful after all?

Hearing of the party's impossible success at integrating lycanthropes into Wolfhaven, a letter was left by the party asking them to speak at his door that had magically appeared on the tree in the center of the village. Once inside, he explained that he wanted to help them with their "bastion woes" and assist them with getting Wolfhaven more set up to handle the new lycanthrope residents. All he wanted in exchange was for them to sign over their portion of the Phandelver Mine, claiming that he wanted access to the magical items that might be uncovered within.

However, for once he seemed to meet his match with Gaelin stepping in to make a counter-offer: sponsor their group, becoming part of it to get the artefacts he wants while leaving the mine still in the group's name. With a flick of his tail and quick magical contract drawn up, he agreed and with Gaelin's signature their partnership became official.

With their alliance confirmed, Vasuki was willing to not only help transport the bastion followers and their equipment, but for a small fee also provided materials to house them and promised to "assist where he could" with the buildings. And he was even willing to bring a few of the spiders over from Thundertree and start a new nest with a fresh queen egg! As long as the party had somewhere safe for them to nest (would be bad for business to have them in town eating villagers!)

Not only was he willing to assist with the bastions, but it seems he might be getting a soft spot for them as he also offered the party a very unique Sending Stone that they could use to contact him whenever he is free. Plus, for a nominal fee, he would be willing to transport them between wherever they are and Wolfhaven and back!

All he asks if to be allowed to establish a more permanent location in Wolfhaven and be allowed to study the lycanthropes and giant spiders. As well the party themselves of course, though perhaps he didn't say that part too loudly.
