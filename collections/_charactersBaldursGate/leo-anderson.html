The hardworking Guildmaster of the Andoril Fighter's Guild, he is a kind but firm individual who manages the guild by ensuring jobs are accepted with enough information, organising supplies for new adventurers, and generally ensuring the day-to-day running of the place goes without issue. He rarely interacts with individuals - preferring to delegate to those under him - but can nearly always be found in the Guildermaster's office, pouring over paperwork and managing resource requisitions.

There is only one thing that seems to upset Leo: his name. He is very conscious of his name meaning lion and will snap at anyone that tries to make fun of it.

"I am well aware of my name's meaning. Yes I hate my father for thinking he was so bloody smart about it, no I don't talk to him anymore, no I won't explain any more than that. Got it? Good, don't bring it up again."
