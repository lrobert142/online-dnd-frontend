Rasker is a very strange birdfolk who adores experimenting with all kinds of alchemical reagents, strange chemical reactions, and developing unique potions of questionable usefulness. As the owner of The Caw-ldron, he will gladly buy anything adventurer’s find that might be useful in a potion, even if it’s likely to result in an inert or otherwise useless (or rarely, harmful) brew. This is because he adores experimentation and does not see failure as a bad thing, but as a means to success:

“I didn’t fail, I succeeded in learning a new way not to make it work!”

Despite his unkempt looks and eccentric demeanour, he is quite harmless and simply enjoys life to the best of his ability. Besides, his normal run-of-the-mill potions are actually quite useful and cost-effective, and many adventurers owe their lives to his well-made Healing Potions; they even taste a little fruity!
