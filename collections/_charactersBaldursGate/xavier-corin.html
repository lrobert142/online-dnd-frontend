A respectful, kindly gentleman who maintains the Baldur's Gate Harper’s Hall and ensures everyone who resides in it is looked after. As the chief custodian, he is also responsible for ensuring everything is well stocked, keeping the peace, and making sure people don’t overstay their welcome. He isn't particularly fond of listening to the stories of adventurers - given how many he's already heard, and how many are drastically exaggerated - but is kind enough to pretend to listen anyway.

Apparently he is quite the dangerous swordsman, although his natural charisma is able to disarm nearly any situation meaning he rarely resorts to violence so nobody can confirm this.
