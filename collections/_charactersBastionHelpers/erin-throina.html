Though a devout priestess of Waukeen, she believes in supporting all kinds of religious beliefs and helping guide others through difficult times. Upon hearing that the party was planning to establish roots in Thundertree, she asked Gaelin to be allowed to establish a place of worship there to give others hope in times of need.

"Religion and belief is strong, it brings the gods protection to us and makes us strong. With the god's watching over us, we will make Thundertree a safe and kind place."
