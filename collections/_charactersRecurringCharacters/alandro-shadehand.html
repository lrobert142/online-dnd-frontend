Dangerous, mysterious, ruthlessly efficient, sadistic. These are only a few of the words that can be used to describe the master assassin that is Alandro. And he revels in them.

A killer since birth, he was raised by the Shadehand Clan in the ways of stealth, subterfuge, and murder from the moment he could wield a blade and took to his grim assignments with a devotion and exhilaration that cannot properly be put into words. He seems to be particularly fond of poisons and disguises and is not afraid to utilise even the most 'cowardly' of tactics if it means getting close enough to his targets to take them out; even willing to disguise himself as loved ones of his target so he can get close enough to run his blade across their throat.

The blade he wields is enchanted to prevent the scars it leaves from ever healing properly even when cured with magic or powerful potions. Additionally, the poisons he uses seem to be custom-made variants of other widely known poisons, being much more potent and able to combine multiple effects - such as paralysis and blindness - although how this is achieved is as-of-yet unknown.

The only constant known to his mannerisms and disguises is that he is left-handed, a somewhat rarity in the world.
