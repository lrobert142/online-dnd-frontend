Petee and Gaelin are sent together on a new adventure to assist the frontier town of Phandalin.

While the Baldur's Gate Adventurer's Guild, Petee and Gaelin - two new adventurers who've not yet had the chance to meet each other - are matched up together on a new job; to investigate Phandalin and assist the Rockseeker brothers in finding the Lost Mine of Phandelver. A new journey begins, and with it a new story is ready to unfold.
