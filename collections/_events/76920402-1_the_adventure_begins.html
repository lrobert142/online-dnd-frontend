The party arrives at the site of a goblin ambush, ready to fight and begin making a name for themselves.

After a long uneventful journey from Baldur's Gate, the party finally arrives just outside Phandalin. But not all is as its seems as they are ambushed by a roaming band of goblins. Though easy to beat, some managed to escape into the nearby forest. The party moves to track them down, they must be stopped!
