The goblins were ready for the party's second attack and were able to ambush them and capture them!

Unfortunately the party's second assault on the goblin cave was cut short when the bugbear leader ambushed them and pushed them to death's door. It was only by the 'kindness' of a certain goblin - Yeemik - that they were captured instead of killed. Of course, Yeemik only wanted to enslave them to plot to overthrow Klarg, but perhaps the party can use his arrogance to their advantage.

They also encountered a human who had been captured and tormented by the goblins, a strange young man who called himself Sildar Hallwinter.
