Having sewn the seeds of rebellion, the time has finally come to cut down their captors and escape!

After having sewn the seeds of mistrust amongst the goblins of the cave, the party is able to use the chaos to slay Klarg, put an end to Yeemik, retrieve their items, save Sildar, free the remaining wolves, and escape! All in day's work.

Sewing so much chaos has completely dismantled the goblin operation and those lucky enough to survive has fled to the surrounding area. Without a leader, they are unlikely to cause much trouble.
