Having been saved from the goblins, the young wolf wants to join the party.

The wolf that Klarg kept under his foot was finally freed and able to return to his pack. However, he felt a certain fondness for Petee and made the decision to stay by his side and join the party for future adventures.

Petee gave him the name Brutus, which he seems very happy with.
