A strange, tall, pure white wolfman from the Feywild joins the party!

The party met a very lost-looking white wolf while travelling through Phandalin and upon learning of his good nature and desire to have an adventure on the Material Plane - as they were from the Feywild - asked him to join them on their adventures.
