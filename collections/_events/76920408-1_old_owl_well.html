--- identifier: "80c86ba0-aef1-4a97-826a-863df5da5893" title: "Old Owl Well" day: 8 month: 4 year: 7692 ---

A horrible encounter with a twisted necromancer ends with their death and an undead plague purged.

Learning of a powerful necromancer in the area, the party assaults the decrepit ruined tower of Old Owl Well. Fighting through a hoard of undead, they found the necromancer responsible, a nasty Red Mage by the name of Hamun Kost. After soothing the Banshee of the well and tricking the necromancer close, they were able to slay him and put an end to his disturbing actions, freeing the area from his undead influence.
