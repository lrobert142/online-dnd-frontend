The party fights through Thundertree, slaying a dragon and putting a stop to Saren's Simulacrum.

Arriving in Thundertree, the parth fights through a hoard of animated plants and zombies, even helping some friendly spiders save their home from some Underdark invaders. After saving the spiders, the party fought a dragon named Venomfang, interrupted a dark ritual being held by Saren, and slayed her to prevent her from further corrupting the land! However, it turned out that Saren was only a Simulacrum, raising many concerns about why a Divine priestess had an Arcane double.

After saving the town and quieting the residents, the party left with more questions than answers.
