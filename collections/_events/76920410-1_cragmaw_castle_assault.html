Learning about the capture of Gundren Rockseeker, the party assaults Cragmaw Castle to save them.

Having finally tracked down the whereabouts of the dwarf Gundren Rockseeker, the party begins a tactical assault on Cragmaw Castle to save him from his captors. Though filled with aggressive goblins and bugbears aplenty, the party was not dissuaded and was able to successfully safely extract Gundren and flee from the returning hunting party.
