While returning to Phandalin, a she-wolf joins the party, enamoured by Kaldoris

As the party returned to Phandalin with Gundren in tow, they encountered a group of frinedly wolves. Some of the wolves were from the pack they freed from the goblins many days ago and seems excited to see them. One of the female wolves seemed oddly taken with Kaldoris and insisted on traveling with them, leaving her old pack with their blessing and traveling with the party.
