--- identifier: "1787f0ec-09e4-4e1a-b051-77cb37a9e76a" title: "Goblin Emancipation" day: 14 month: 4 year: 7692 ---

While patching their wounds, the party encounters three goblin slaves and is able to free them from their chains.

While recovering their strength from a recent battle in the Mine, the party encountered a small goblin slave, Moglin who was part of a trio being forced to work in harsh conditions for The Black Spider. With kind words and natural charisma, the party was able to convince the scared goblin that they were friends and were led back to the other two goblins. More quick thinking and a kindness beyond measure allowed the party to save the other two as well and break their shackles to free them from their life of slavery.

For their kindness, the goblins swore to help the party in any way they could and hailed them as saints and heroes for their actions. As the goblins fled to safety and freedom, the party felt a warmth wash over them for their selfless actions.
