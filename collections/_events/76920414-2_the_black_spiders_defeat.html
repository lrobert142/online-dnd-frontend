The Black Spider has fallen! The Phandelver Mine is freed, and Phandalin is saved!

After a long journey, numerous difficulties, and a tough final battle, Nezzar The Black Spider has been captured! Though their cowardice nearly allowed them to flee, the party was able to stop his escape and take him down, freeing the mine from his control and putting an end to whatever malevolent plans he may have had.

In addition, the party was able to find Nundro and with quick thinking and a steady hand from Gaelin, was able to save his life from the spider egg that had been implanted into his chest. Though injured and mourning the loss of his brother, Tharden, Nundro is glad to be alive and eager to meet up with his other brother, Gundren. Finally he will be free from this nightmare.
