With a heavy heart, the party returns to Phandalin with Tharden's corpse, ready to give him a proper send off.

The party returns to Phandalin with Tharden's body in tow and informs Gundren of the unfortunate news. Gundren is heartbroken and immediately swear off drinking and insists that his brother must be given a proper funeral pyre to free his spirit and let him be at rest.

Thankful that his other brother, Nundro, is back safe and sound. He laments not being able to have saved them both and wishes things could have been better. The ceremony is touching and though it will take them both time to fully heal, they are glad the party was able to grant them this kindness and closure.
