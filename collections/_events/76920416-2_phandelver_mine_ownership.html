As thanks for all the party has done for Gundren and for all of Phandalin, the party is given partial ownership of the mine.

To begin repaying the kindness of the party for everything they have done, Gundren and Nundro offer the party partial ownership of the Phandelver Mine. Though the brothers will manage the day-to-day runnings, setup, and selling of materials, they offer the party a cut of the profits as ongoing thanks for everything they have done.

However, it will take the brothers nearly two tendays to fully get the mine up and running and so the party will have to wait until that is sorted before they can give them their first payout.
