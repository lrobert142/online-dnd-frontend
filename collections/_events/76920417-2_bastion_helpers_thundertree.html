Hearing of the Heroes of Phandalin, several people wished to establish a base of operations to assist the party in their future endeavours.

With the success of the party came great respect and desire for others to help these up-and-coming heroes. Several individuals reached out to establish a secure bastion of hope for the party to use as a base of operations in the future. The party was kind enough to accept their help and even offered Thundertree as a location to begin setting up.

Though just ruins at the time, they were more than willing to put effort into restoring the ruins and making a safe and secure bastion for the party, at no cost to the party of course. However, it would be a lot of work to break down the old buildings and establish new ones and would likely take at least a month before things would be up and running.
