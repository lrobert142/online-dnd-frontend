An ambush by gnolls nearly ended Sildar's life, however thanks to quick thinking by Petee and the party, his life was saved!

Stopping to make camp for the night, gnolls descended on the weary party and quickly attempted to lay waste to them and their travelling companions. Although they could do little against the party itself, they were able to sneak up on Sildar and bring him to death's door! However, before the gnoll could strike the final fatal blow, Petee's quick thinking ensured his survival and the summary execution of the would-be gnoll killer.

Thankful beyond words, Sildar promised to always have the party's back as this made the second time the party had saved his life.
