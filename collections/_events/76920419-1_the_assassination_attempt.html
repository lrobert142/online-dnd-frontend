An assassin was amongst the party's ranks! Though thankfully unsuccessful, the attempt on Petee's life has left scars; both phsyical and emotional.

After enduring a dangerous battle with harpies and a cyclops, Petee was tricked away from the party by an assassin using the face of one of their travel companions, Elana. Once alone, the assassin captured them, ransacked their belongings, and slit their thorat to leave them for dead!

This assassin - Alandro, sent by Goldblood and known by Gaelin - would have succeeded if not for the incredible senses and quick acting of Brutus who was able to lead the rest of their party to their side and bring them back from the brink.

The party was clearly shaken - especially when the real Elana returned - but the attack served to strengthen their resolve to see Goldblood brought to justice for her terrible crimes and cowardice. Nothing can stop them now!
