A swift exit after a short stay, the party can no longer afford to stay in this grand city.

After speaking with Sildar, they learn that the Lord's Alliance has already been informed by Goldblood about their supposed betrayal and will be arrested the soon, but should be able to stay a few days as long as they stay quiet and undercover. Only to learn that Gaelin and Sýnchysi had previously made quite the commotion in the market district. Without the ability to remain under the radar, the party must think of an alternative plan, and quick!

After some deliberation, the party decides to stage an escape but pretending to wound Sildar before escaping on horseback south to Tsendoric. Meanwhile they will double back and head north to Wolfhaven and Malgalus where they will be safer from prying eyes until this whole ordeal can be smoothed over.

With horses secured and a masterful performance by Sildar and Gaelin, the party is able to flee the city and trick their pursuers into heading south while they sneak off to the north.
