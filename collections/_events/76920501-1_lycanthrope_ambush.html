--- identifier: "e99c8969-331e-4f72-8ec4-d6ff53f04714" title: "Lycanthrope Ambush" day: 1 month: 5 year: 7692 ---

The party is ambushed by a group of roaming lycanthropes! Thankfully one of them recognises Petee and the attack is stopped.

Though initially tense, one of the lycanthropes knows that Petee is Peet's son and indicates they are allies to them and the town of Wolfhaven. They apologise for scaring them and ask to speak with the party at a later date to better explain themselves, before leaving the party to finish their rest under the light of the full moon.
