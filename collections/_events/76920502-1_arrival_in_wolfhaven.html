The party arrives at Wolfhaven, a sleepy little village that keeps to itself.

The party spends some time getting to know the locals, Petee catches up with his father, Peet, and everyone checks in at the only inn in town, meeting Mango as well! It feels good to finally be off the road and back in a peaceful location.

Although there is still much to do, the party is glad to have a moment to breath in the safety of a village.
