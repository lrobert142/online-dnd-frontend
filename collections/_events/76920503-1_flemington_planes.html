The party investigates the nearby village of Flemington Planes and witnesses the horror enacted on the town.

Having heard of a recent attack on a neighbouring village of Flemington Planes, the party travels to it to learn what had happened. Upon arrival they see the horrible truth: it had been attacked by cultists who defiled the town and left no survivors.

The horror was palpable, the attack disgusting, and the pain of the town felt again through the party. Their anger was channelled to resolve to bring an end to these horrible villains.
