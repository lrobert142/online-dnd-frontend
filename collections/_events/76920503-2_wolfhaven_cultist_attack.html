Returning to Wolfhaven, the party is just in time to witness the cultists trying to attack the village!

Arriving back in town after the horrifying discoveries in Flemington Planes, the party witnesses Mango being attacked and killed by the very same cultists! They seek to raze the town and slaughter everyone in it, but the party is strong and able to fend them off with the help of their lycanthrope ally, Darax.

At the last moment, the party is able to revive Mango from otherwise permanent death and bring her back to her beloved. Though thankful, she - like the rest of the village - is terrified of the lycanthrope and quickly hides inside. Now the party is left with a new issues, how do they deal with the reveal of lycanthropes to the town, and how will they stop them being hunted and feared?
