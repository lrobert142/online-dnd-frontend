--- identifier: "671c7256-a863-41f8-849f-2a14b14a4b58" title: "Acceptance of Lycanthropes" day: 4 month: 5 year: 7692 ---

The party manages to do the impossible and have Wolfhaven accept the lycanthropes into their village.

Through a combination of reverent sermons, powerful stories, incredible decorations, and wonderful food, a celebration is held to welcome the lycanthropes into Wolfhaven as friends and allies, dispelling the fear around them and allowing them to coexist in peace. Vargus' pack is able to walk amongst non-lycanthropes for the first time in remembered history and be seen as equals instead of feared as monsters.

A truly historic event, Vargus swears to the party they will do whatever they can to assist them in any way they can. After all, they finally no longer have to live in fear in the shadows, afraid of being hunted.
