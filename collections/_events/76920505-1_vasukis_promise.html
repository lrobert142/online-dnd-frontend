Vasuki is very interested in the party and wants to make a deal with them that will benefit them both.

After his door appears on the tree in Wolfhaven, the party interacts with Vasuki who makes an offer to work alongside them in exchange for them signing over their part of the Phandelver Mine to him. However, Gaelin is able to convince him to become business partner's with them instead, thereby granting all of them long-term benefits. Vasuki agrees, and a contract is signed to work together.

In addition to working together, Vasuki is willing to help teleport the current bastion helpers from Thundertree to Wolfhaven, and even help them secure building materials to set up homes for them in exchange for a small fee. After receiving the gold he explains that with his magic and the lycanthrope's help it should only take 2-3 days to extablish the houses and get them set up again.

Furthermore, Vasuki offers them a custom Sending Stone that they may use if they ever wish to be teleported back to Wolfhaven - to check on their bastions, interact with friends, or even just visit. Provided they can pay the toll, of course, teleportation isn't cheap!

Before leaving, Sync asks about bringing the spiders from Thundertree over to Wolfhaven. Vasuki says bringing them too close to the town would be dangerous, and he cannot bring them all, but he offers a queen spider egg to establish a new colony if they can find somewhere for them to live safely. Sync says he will look into it, and Vasuki says he will wait until the party gives the OK.
