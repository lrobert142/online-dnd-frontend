After a long and difficult battle, Stalerock Fort was finally cleansed of the Bhaalist invasion. Although there were no survivors, the party was able to ensure their sacrifices were not in vain, and gave them a proper burial. Captain Joanna was also laid to rest with her soldiers.

Additionally, the party was able to secure the artefact of the fort and ensure it didn't fall into enemy hands.
