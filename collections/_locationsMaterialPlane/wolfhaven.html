A tiny and quiet village that was established by a retired adventurer - Pete "Peet" McBuggins - nearly 30 years ago when he stepped away from his duties to settle back into a simple lifestyle. Though originally just one house and a simple field, its proximity to the Andoril-Malgalus trade route eventually led to more people setting up in the area until it became the small village it is today.

Growing a small variety of hardy vegetables and grains, and hunting the plentiful animals in the surrounding forests, the village is entirely self-sufficient though does sometimes trade with passing caravans to obtain more exotic or difficult-to-make goods.

They seem to prefer hunting alongside animals, particularly wolves and other canines.

Upon camping in the forest nearby Wolfhaven they learned of a pack of lycanthropes nearby. However, they were not hostile and instead seemed to be safeguarding the village from evildoers, including cultists that had been attacking other nearby villages and now seemed to be trying to attack Wolfhaven itself.

The town was attacked by cultists who managed to sneak in under the guide or travellers! However, thanks to quick thinking and swift actions, the party was able to dispatch the cultists and the abomindation they attempted to summon, even using Peet's old Scroll of Revivify to bring back Mango. However, it required the help of a lycanthrope, Darax, and now the whole town is aware of their presence!

The party was able to gather allies in the village and create a celebration for the lycanthropes, painting them in a positive light and convincing the residents that they are protectors and saviours, not vicious monsters. By helping with a relgious sermon, creating a moving and powerful tale, sharing carved gifts, and providing food and drinks in a massive celebration of togetherness and diversity, the village came together with the lycanthropes as friends and agreed to live alongside them.

Deciding to move their bastions from Thundertree to somewhere more familiar, the party has made a deal with Vasuki to bring their followers and friends into the village, bringing much more security, people, and complexities. However, despite the difficulties, nobody can deny how good it will be to have more allies join this once tiny village.
