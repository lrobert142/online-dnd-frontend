Lycanthropy and its dangers

A retrospective by Franz A. Jauren, Castvale, 7581ATS

Lycanthropy and those who spread it - known as werewolves - are dangerous! It causes insatiable bloodlust, unrestrained anger, a dark desire to consume human flesh, and brutality unmatched by any other creature. Those who fall to this terrible curse are rendered little more than savage beasts that pose a substantial threat to all around them and have been known to slaughter entire villages singlehandedly. Under no circumstances should you underestimate the visciousness and cunning of a werewolf.

The spread of the curse

Lycanthropy is typically spread by a bite from an afflicted creature, however it is also possible for it to be spread via other bodily fluids such as saliva or blood. It is for this very reason that when fighting a werewolf - which, this author must stress, should be avoided at all costs - it is vitally important to cover wounds as well as any open orifices such as the mouth and nose, and when possible fight at a distance to minimise the chance of blood and viscera splashback in combat. Even a single drop of their tainted blood can carry the full effect of the curse! Be wary when fighting these terrible creatures.

A cure?

This author wishes to make one thing very clear: if you are infected with lycanthropy, make all haste to a healer immediately. Do not delay, do not tarry, do not put it off, and always always always insist on a double-dose!

Once one is infected with the lycanthropic curse, they have no more than 72 hours to seek out a cure before the effect becomes permanent. No amount of magic, potions, or prayers can undo this effect as it seeps into soul of an individual and irreversibly taints their very being. It has been suggested that a powerful Wish spell may be able to undo this effect, however no magic user has ever seen the need to waste such a powerful spell on such a pathetic creature; 'tis far simpler to simply put the savage beast down.

While cures for early-stage lycanthropy are expensive - involving sanctified silver, Wolfsbane, and numerous difficult to retrieve ingredients - its cost is always worth it and afflicted individuals are encouraged to take out whatever loans necessary to secure it. Should this prove impossible for whatever reason, it is of the utmost importance that the afflicted individual report immediately to the highest ranking member of security available so that they may be swiftly and humanely euthanized before the curse can completely consume them. Your loved ones will tahnk you for your selfless sacrifce.

It may also be possible for spells such as Remove Curse to cure an individual as well, however the success rate in these instances is significantly reduced.

Hunting werewolves

While werewolves are strong, nimble, resiliant, and incredibly dangerous, they are not immortal and can be put down like any other monster. While conventional weapons will do little against a werewolf - indeed, reports of older werewolves being completely immune to conventional weapons have been verified as truth - they are two major weaknesses: Wolfsbane and Silver.

Remember, many adventures, alchemists, and guardsmen died to bring you this knowledge. Don't let their sacrifices be in vain!

Wolfsbane

While not as effective as silver, the herb known as Wolfsbane is extremely common and available in a wide variety of forms, from the herb itself to a distilled liquid that can be splashed upon assailants, even many healing potions used it as a foundation! Burning the herb ofttimes results in weakening a werewolf, potentially giving you enough time to flee to someone carrying silver who can put the beast down.

If you cannot burn the herb, tossing a liquid containing Wolfsbane - even a healing potion containing it as a base - can make a werewolf very sick and in some cases even blind it if you can get it into its eyes. Remember, Wolfsbane will not kill a werewolf, but it can weaken it long enough to find someone who can, so use every tool in your arsenal to drive back these creatures!

Silver

Silver is death to a werewolf. It cripples their resiliance, removes their regeneration, and leaves them as little more than easy kills. If possible, coat your weapons in a thin coating of silver or, if one can afford the increased price for the blessing, santified silver. Striking with a weapon coated in this, even a simple dagger, can bring even the most nasty and viscious werewolf too its knees for an easy execution.

If you cannot afford silver, do not be afraid to arm yourself with a silver candlestick, silver poker, or indeed even a silver coin! All forms of silver will burn a werewolf upon contact, and could give you the precious time you need to escape.

Werewolves vs Lycanthropes

Werewolves are viscious beasts without a mind of their own fit only for being eradicated from the world. Lycanthropes are infected individuals of sound mind able to integrate with society without issues. This is a belief amongst some individuals who wish to make the distinction between the creatures.

This author wishes to put an end to this hearsay nonsense and make one thing clear: werewolves and lycanthropes are the same thing - cruel sadistic monsters who exist only to have their filth wiped from this world! If you hear anyone saying anything like this - wishing to distinguish the creatures, offering refuge for them, speaking about them as anything other than the cruel monsters they are - it is your duty as a citizen of Quixus to immediately report them to a member of authority so their lies may be quashed.

As all readers are no doubt aware, the recent horrifying werewolf invasion left many towns decimated, thousands killed, and many more families and lives irreversibly ruined due to their savage tendencies! Anyone conspiring or sympathising with these creatures must be reported for treason immediately!
