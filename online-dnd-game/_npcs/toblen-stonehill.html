The boisterous and energetic owner of the Stonehill Inn in Phandalin. Always there with a caring ear, an uplifting joke, and a strong drink, he can be relied upon to raise your spirits in any circumstance. Despite his overly flirtatious nature, he is actually quite the gentleman and only means them as a bit of harmless fun.

Incredibly thankful to the party for helping free the town from control of the Redbrands, he makes sure to always keep a few rooms prepared for them.
